How to play

  1. The game starts with a deck of face-down cards on the board.
  2. Click on any card to reveal its face.
  3. Click on another card to find a matching pair.
  4. If the cards match, they remain face-up on the board.
  5. If the cards do not match, they will be turned back face-down.
  6. Continue flipping cards and finding pairs until all cards are matched.
  7. The game keeps track of your time taken to complete the game.
